Pulse and Arc hosted in the cloud

Cubewise Cloud provides Pulse and Arc hosted in the cloud and is the recommended way to use these products in IBM Planning Analytics SaaS environments.

Access your Pulse and Arc environment from anywhere — better administer and develop all your IBM PA SaaS environments without needing on-premise infrastructure.

How does it work?

  • 24/7 access to Pulse and Arc connected to all your IBM PA SaaS environments
  • Real-time monitoring — issues are caught as they arise, leading to faster resolutions
  • Scheduled upgrades — get the latest features as soon as they are available
  • Cubewise best practices — your products are always configured to the highest standard
  • Pulse and Arc do not store cube data — only metadata such as sessions and change tracking is stored in your Cubewise Cloud environment
