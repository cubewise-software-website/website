You decide when to upgrade, how to configure, and who has access — no shared infrastructure, no external dependencies.

All metadata and configuration lives within your own network. No cube data ever leaves your environment.

Fits your environment

Integrates with your existing Active Directory, SSO, and internal security policies straight out of the box.
