Understand

Deep insight into your TM1 models — dependencies, documentation, and change history.

Relationship Diagrams

Generate relationship diagrams from your models to understand how objects interact. Output diagrams to PDF for printing and distribution, or use the interactive diagrams in the web interface.

Interactive Model Navigation

View and interact with documentation of your model by clicking on each object and their relationships. Rules and processes can be easily viewed in Pulse with intuitive navigation features.

Technical Documentation

Produce technical documentation at the click of a button. Pulse interrogates your TM1 model and extracts important information about each object — cubes, dimensions, and processes.

Licence Optimisation Reports

Optimise your investment in IBM Planning Analytics using the built-in licence reports. Ensure compliance with your current licences, or identify users who aren't using the system so licences can be reallocated.

System Validation

Built-in rules identify common pitfalls that may slow the system or make support difficult. Naming conventions are also validated, helping you build consistent and easy-to-maintain systems.

Troubleshoot Errors

Quickly identify and diagnose errors across all TM1 instances. Historical error data is searchable and filterable, making it easy to find the root cause of issues fast.

Historical Data Second by Second

All detailed data is accessible second by second, giving you granular visibility into system performance over time. Look back at any earlier state to diagnose a problem.

Build Your Own Dashboards

The Pulse Explorer feature lets you build custom dashboards and reports. A set of ready-made dashboards is included to help you get started.
