Pulse

Download Pulse

Access the latest version of Pulse — available exclusively to Pulse customers. If you do not have access, please reach out to your local Cubewise contact or contact us through the website.

  • Available for Windows Server
  • Connects to IBM Planning Analytics on-premises and SaaS
  • Browser-based — no client installation required for end users
  • Supports IBM Planning Analytics hierarchies and Debugging
Download Pulse

By downloading you agree to the license terms below.

Get started in 3 steps

  1. 1
    Download & install

    Run the Pulse installer on your Windows Server. The setup wizard guides you through the process.

  2. 2
    Connect to your TM1 servers

    Add your IBM Planning Analytics on-premises or SaaS environments. Pulse auto-discovers local TM1 instances.

  3. 3
    Start monitoring

    Open Pulse in any browser and get full visibility into your TM1 environment — sessions, threads, chores, and more.
