Aerospace & Defence

How Pattonair reduced TM1 user locks and gained 24/7 visibility

"Pulse has helped us to significantly reduce user locks and allowed us to be notified via email if any user is locked in TM1 for more than 5 minutes."

About Pattonair

Pattonair is the leading global aerospace and defence supply chain service provider. Their TM1 model is core to business processes, accessed throughout the globe and required to be available near to 24/7. The model connects to hundreds of data sources, with loading processes required to run across multiple TM1 servers — daily and in some cases multiple times per day on demand.

The Challenge

With TM1 models accessed globally around the clock, Pattonair faced persistent challenges with user locks — and had no effective way to monitor users or be alerted when locks occurred. The scale and complexity of their environment meant performance issues were difficult to diagnose, and tracking which users were actively using the system versus working offline was impossible without the right tools.

The Solution

Pattonair implemented Pulse to gain full visibility into their TM1 environment. Pulse's alert system enabled email notifications whenever a user was locked in TM1 for more than 5 minutes — allowing the team to act on issues before they became business problems. The monitoring capability also gave insight into Perspectives report usage, enabling the team to plan migrations to a more centralised and standardised reporting solution.

The Results

  • Significantly reduced user locks through targeted performance enhancements informed by Pulse monitoring
  • Identified the most frequent users and those still working offline — enabling targeted training and knowledge sharing across the finance function
  • Used Pulse to monitor the success of new application deployments by tracking usage frequency
  • Confident that technical documentation for any model can be produced on demand through Pulse
  • Ability to monitor and track changes across production and development environments simultaneously
  • Can roll back any mistake without restoring from backup or taking servers offline
