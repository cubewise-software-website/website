Monitor, understand and manage IBM Planning Analytics

Pulse gives you complete visibility into what's happening across all your TM1 servers — so you can act fast and stay in control.

24/7 monitoring & alerting

Real-time notifications for user locks, chore failures, memory thresholds, and model changes — delivered by email or integrated into your alerting platform.

Audit trails

A complete history of user activity, object changes, and chore executions. Know exactly what changed, when it changed, and who changed it.

Live migration

Deploy changes to production without taking servers offline. Pulse migrates packages live, with a full history and the ability to roll back if something goes wrong.

Performance analysis

Identify slow chores, memory spikes, and usage patterns that impact performance. Continuously improve uptime and keep your users productive.

User activity analysis

Understand how users interact with your TM1 applications. Identify who needs training and surface usage patterns that impact your model's performance.

Change tracking

Every change to a TM1 object is tracked and logged. Verify that correct changes were made in production and quickly identify the source of any issue.

Governance and control over what goes into production

Arc+ helps administrators enforce standards, manage deployments, and maintain the integrity of your IBM Planning Analytics environment.

Best practice rules

Define coding and naming standards for your team. Arc+ automatically checks compliance and flags violations before they reach production.

Deployment packages

All object dependencies are automatically resolved. Administrators get a complete picture of what's being deployed before approving any migration.

Separation of duties

Keep TM1 development and production operations separate. Arc+ supports approval workflows so administrators stay in control of what changes in production.

Rollback support

Full deployment history with downloadable migration logs. If a deployment causes issues, roll back changes quickly without manual intervention.

Approval workflows

Route every deployment through configurable approval steps. Reviewers see exactly what's changing and sign off before anything reaches production.

Full deployment history

Every release is timestamped and attributed. Pull up any past deployment, compare versions, and answer compliance questions with documented evidence.
