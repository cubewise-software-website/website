Governance and control over what goes into production

Arc+ helps administrators enforce standards, manage deployments, and maintain the integrity of your IBM Planning Analytics environment.

Best practice rules

Define coding and naming standards for your team. Arc+ automatically checks compliance and flags violations before they reach production.

Deployment packages

All object dependencies are automatically resolved. Administrators get a complete picture of what's being deployed before approving any migration.

Separation of duties

Keep TM1 development and production operations separate. Arc+ supports approval workflows so administrators stay in control of what changes in production.

Rollback support

Full deployment history with downloadable migration logs. If a deployment causes issues, roll back changes quickly without manual intervention.

Approval workflows

Route every deployment through configurable approval steps. Reviewers see exactly what's changing and sign off before anything reaches production.

Full deployment history

Every release is timestamped and attributed. Pull up any past deployment, compare versions, and answer compliance questions with documented evidence.
