A modern Excel experience for IBM Planning Analytics

Slice gives Finance teams all the power of IBM Planning Analytics directly in Excel — with the features that Perspectives never had.

Full TM1 Excel compatibility

Support for all TM1 Excel functions including DBRW, DBR, DBRA, and more — with full hierarchy support across all formula types.

Dynamic columns & cross-drill

Create reports with dynamic column structures that automatically expand with your hierarchy. Cross-drill from summary to detail without leaving your workbook.

Cube Viewer & Subset Editor

A full Cube Viewer and Subset Editor built directly into Excel — no need to switch to another application when you want to explore your data.

Sandbox & writeback

Create sandboxes and write data back to IBM Planning Analytics directly from your Excel workbook. Plan and scenario-model without affecting live data.

Drill-through & annotations

Drill through to source data from any cell and add annotations to document decisions — all without leaving Excel.

Seamless upgrade from Perspectives

Slice is the natural successor to Perspectives. Existing workbooks and templates migrate with virtually no learning curve.

Get your data into IBM Planning Analytics without IT

Atmosphere connects Finance teams to every data source they rely on — ERP, CRM, cloud platforms, and more — with no-code pipelines Finance can own and run.

34+ pre-built connectors

Connect to Dynamics 365, NetSuite, SAP, Snowflake, Databricks, Salesforce, and many more — with connectors that understand your GL and ERP structures out of the box.

Finance-owned schedules

Set up pipelines on your own schedule and update them when your requirements change — without raising IT tickets or waiting for developer support.

Two-way data pipelines

Push plan data and business structures back to downstream systems automatically — keeping BI, data warehouses, and operational systems aligned with the latest actuals and forecasts.

Secure by design

All data is encrypted in-transit. No data is stored by Atmosphere. Enterprise-grade access controls ensure the right people see the right data.

IBM Planning Analytics data in Microsoft Power BI

PowerConnect provides a live, secure connection from your TM1 cubes to Power BI — so your dashboards always reflect current data without manual exports.

Live TM1 connection

Connect Power BI Desktop and Power BI Service directly to your IBM Planning Analytics cubes — on-premises, cloud, or hybrid — for always-current reports.

Automatic dimension sync

IBM Planning Analytics hierarchies and dimensions sync automatically to Power BI. No manual updates when your model changes — reports always reflect the latest structure.

Security integration

PowerConnect integrates with your existing IBM Planning Analytics security model — so users only see the data they're entitled to, both in TM1 and in Power BI.

Ad-hoc & scheduled refresh

Refresh data on demand or on a schedule. Works with Power BI Desktop, Service, and mobile — without any manual data exports or file transfers.
