D365 Pipeline

The Atmosphere D365 Pipeline connects to any endpoint in D365 Finance & Operations. It includes predefined endpoints that understand your structures — such as Departments and Accounts — and turns them into clear hierarchies for reporting and planning.

The Pipeline works on its own or alongside Microsoft Fabric and supports real-time refreshes during busy periods like month-end.
