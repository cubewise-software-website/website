Raise standards across your entire implementation

Bedrock brings together information and experience from years of complex TM1 implementations. It addresses the education gap in TM1 by providing standards and methodologies that every developer, administrator, and end user can adopt immediately.

  • 30–50% reduction in TI coding through a functional method approach
  • Dramatically faster prototyping phase
  • 3-mode standardised logging system for auditing and testing
  • Trialled and implemented across numerous highly complex TM1 sites

Built for everyone in your team

Bedrock is designed for anyone involved in creating, implementing, managing, or supporting IBM TM1 systems — from first-time developers to senior administrators.

  • Developers — reduced build time and easier prototyping for a better fit-for-purpose outcome
  • Administrators — more consistent auditing, standardised code, and reduced succession risk
  • End users — a flatter learning curve makes TM1 simpler to develop and administrate
  • IT managers — lower administration costs and less “black box” risk

Free and open source

Bedrock is not a commercial product. It exists solely to increase TM1 knowledge and standards so that every implementer — whether professional services or end user — can drive better results.

  • Free to use, copy, modify, and distribute (with attribution)
  • Source code hosted on GitHub — bug reports and pull requests welcome
  • All code and documents provided without warranty
  • Actively maintained with regular releases
