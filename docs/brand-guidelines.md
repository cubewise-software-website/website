## Colour Palette

Orange Gold is the central colour of the Cubewise brand. It is an energetic and powerful colour inspired by the Mandalorian universe — a nod to the "The DevOps Way" motto adopted by the Cubewise Code team, itself a reference to *"This is the way"*. It is also a homage to the black-and-white printers of the 1980s, the decade TM1 was born.

### Primary

| Name | Hex | RGB | CMYK | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Orange Gold | `#F78E20` | R247 G142 B32 | C0 M43 Y87 K3 | Primary brand colour, CTAs, highlights, accents |

### Secondary

| Name | Hex | RGB | CMYK | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sand Gold | `#FADEC1` | R250 G222 B193 | C0 M11 Y23 K2 | Complements Orange Gold, adds depth; named after Mandalore, the desert home world |
| Charcoal | `#434B53` | R67 G75 B83 | C73 M61 Y51 K35 | Bridge between primary and backgrounds; never used on Dark Night backgrounds |

### Backgrounds

| Name | Hex | RGB | CMYK | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|

| Light Gray | `#F1F2F2` | R241 G242 B242 | C0 M0 Y0 K5 | Secondary light background, cards |
| Dark Night | `#202E3A` | R32 G46 B58 | C84 M69 Y54 K56 | Dark background; Orange Gold used for accents on top of this |

## Voice & Tone

- **Expert but accessible** — we know TM1 deeply; we don't talk down to our audience
- **Direct** — short sentences, active voice, no jargon for jargon's sake
- **Platform-first** — lead with outcomes ("what you can do"), not features ("what it has")

### Word choices

| Prefer | Avoid |
|---|---|
| "component" | "product" / "tool" |
| "Cubewise Platform" | "our software" |
| "IBM Planning Analytics / TM1" | "TM1" alone on first mention |
